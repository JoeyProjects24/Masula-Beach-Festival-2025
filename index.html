About The Festival

Join us for four days of cultural celebrations, culinary delights, and beachside adventures at the beautiful Manginapudi Beach in Machilipatnam.

Dates

June 5 to June 8, 2025

Location

Manginapudi Beach, Machilipatnam, Krishna District, Andhra Pradesh

Timings

6:00 AM to 9:00 PM daily

Free Entry for All

Festival Highlights

Discover the exciting activities and attractions awaiting you at the Masula Beach Festival

Cultural Exhibitions

Traditional dance performances like Kuchipudi, folk art displays, and local crafts like Kalamkari.

Food Stalls

Delicious regional dishes including Bandar Laddu and Bandar Halwa, along with other coastal Andhra specialties.

Adventure Activities

Beach kabaddi, kayaking, paragliding, and other exciting water sports for thrill-seekers.

Special Attraction

Witness history as we prepare a 100-foot dosa aiming for a Guinness World Record!

How to Reach

Plan your journey to the Masula Beach Festival with these transportation options

By Air

The nearest airport is Vijayawada Airport (70 km from Machilipatnam). From there, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach the festival venue.

By Train

The nearest major railway station is Vijayawada Junction (65 km). Regular buses and taxis are available from Vijayawada to Machilipatnam.

By Road

Buses are available from Vijayawada to Machilipatnam. The beach is about 11 km from Machilipatnam town center.
